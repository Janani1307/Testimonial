CHICKEN BRIYANI

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken
  • Oil/Ghee
  • Spices
  • Basmati Rice
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green chillies
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Curry leaves
  • Coriandar leaves
  • Mint leaves
  • Masala's
  • Curd

PROCEDURE

  1. Heat the pan and add oil to the Vessel over medium heat
  2. Add spices into the Vessel
  3. After add onion and saute until it turns brown
  4. Add tomatoes and green chillies,curry leaves,mint leaves and coriander leaves to the pan
  5. Stir and saute for minutes
  6. Then add ginger garlic paste and stir into the mixture in the Vessel
  7. Then saute for a couples of minutes
  8. Add masalas and chicken and saute for few more minutes ,stirring regularly
  9. Add curd to the vessel and mix it
  10. Pour 4 cup of water
  11. After adding water ,add salt to taste.stir everything together,then bring the mixture to a boil
  12. Pour the basmati rice into the boiling water
  13. Add lemon juice and cover the Vessel with tight lid .
  14. Let the rice cook until done
  15. Once the rice is cooked,serve the briyani hot.
